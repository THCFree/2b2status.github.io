2B2Status

A bot that can get 2B2T's chat and events streamed to your minecraft server almost 24/7

Get Started

Commands

Mineraft Commands:
!help | To get the help command!
!!tps | Get the 2B2T TPS!
!follow | Tell the bot to follow you in render distance!
!dupe | Tell the bot to dupe!
!report | Report the user!
!locate | Tell you the location of the bot!
!discord | To get the discord server!
!op | Get "oped" on 2B2T
!come | Tell the bot to come to your coords in render distance!
!stopfollow | Tell the bot to follow you!

Special thanks to startbootstrap.com for the based css and website!
