2B2Status

A bot that can get 2B2T's chat and events streamed to your minecraft server almost 24/7

Get Started

Commands

Minecraft Commands:
!help | To get the help command!
!!tps | Get the 2B2T TPS!
!follow | Tell the bot to follow you in render distance!
!stopfollow | The bot will stop following you!
!dupe | Tell the bot to dupe!
!report | Report the user!
!locate | Tell you the location of the bot!
!discord | To get the discord server!
!op | Get "oped" on 2B2T
!come | Tell the bot to come to your coords in render distance!
!stopfollow | Tell the bot to follow you!
!armor | Tell the bot to wear armor!
!bp | Shows you the best ping that a player have on 2B2T!
!wp | Shows you the worst ping that a player have on 2B2T!
!coinflip | Play coinflip with 2B2Status!
!fact | Give you a random cat's fact!
!gamemode | Give you the gamemode that you wanted!
!owner | Tell you who is the owner/devs!
!ping | Tell you what is your ping on 2B2T!
!rps | Play rock paper scissors with 2B2Status!

2B2T Minecraft Commands:
!joins | SShow you the joins of a specific 2b2t player!
!lastdeath | Show you the latest deaths of a specific player!
!lastkill | Show you the latest kills of a specific player!
!leaves | Show you the leaves of a specific 2b2t player!
!queue | Shows you the queue of 2B2T!
!online | Shows you if a player joined 2B2T/2B2TQueue!
!stats | Shows you the stats of a specific player!
!status | Shows you the status of 2B2T!
